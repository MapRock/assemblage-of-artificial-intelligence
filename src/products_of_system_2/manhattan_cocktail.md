# Classic Manhattan Cocktail Recipe

## Ingredients (1 serving)

- 2 oz rye whiskey *(bourbon can be substituted)*
- 1 oz sweet vermouth
- 2–3 dashes Angostura bitters
- Ice *(for stirring)*
- Maraschino cherry *(for garnish)*

---

## Tools Needed

- Mixing glass  
- Bar spoon *(for stirring)*  
- Jigger *(for measuring)*  
- Strainer  
- Chilled coupe or martini glass  

---

## Instructions

1. Add **2 oz rye whiskey** to a mixing glass.  
2. Add **1 oz sweet vermouth**.  
3. Add **2–3 dashes Angostura bitters**.  
4. Fill the mixing glass with ice.  
5. Stir gently for **20–30 seconds** until well-chilled.  
   - *Avoid shaking to prevent excess dilution and cloudiness.*  
6. Strain into a **chilled coupe or martini glass**.  
7. Garnish with a **maraschino cherry**.

---

## Tips

- Use **high-quality rye** for a spicier profile; bourbon makes the drink sweeter.  
- Stirring time varies by ice size—aim for proper dilution without over-watering.

---

## Variations

- **Dry Manhattan** → Use dry vermouth  
- **Perfect Manhattan** → Use half sweet / half dry vermouth
